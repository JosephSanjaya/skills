# Boost Strategy & SEO Configuration

> Strategic instructions to maximize reach via Medium Publications, the Boost Curation Program, and search engine optimization (SEO).

---

## 1. Curation & Curation Mechanics

Medium utilizes a human-driven nomination system rather than an automated keyword-density algorithm.

- **Nomination Pipeline:** Community curators (selected publication editors) nominate exceptional drafts, which are then approved by Medium's internal editorial team.
- **The Boost Reward:** Nominated stories receive high priority in distribution feeds, generating an immediate 5x to 10x earnings multiplier per read.
- **The Metric: Read Ratio:** The key metric is the *read ratio* (the percentage of users who scroll from the beginning to the absolute end).
  *Rule: Never place self-promotional links or lead magnets at the top or middle of the page. This degrades the read ratio. Place all CTAs at the absolute bottom.*

---

## 2. The 5 Quality Guidelines for Boost

Nominated stories must strictly conform to these five guidelines:

1. **Writer's Experience:** The writer must share unique, lived experiences. Do not write summaries of information found on Google ("write-arounds"). Show direct testing results, metrics, or personal coding incidents.
2. **Value & Impact:** The content must offer a meaningful, educational takeaway that allows the reader to grow.
3. **Respect for the Reader:** Write entirely for the reader's benefit. Keep formatting clean. Avoid spammy affiliate lists or aggressive self-promotion.
4. **Non-Derivative:** The article must present a fresh, original perspective. Do not regurgitate mainstream topics.
5. **Writing and Craftsmanship:** The prose must have polished transitions, professional formatting, and zero grammar/typesetting errors.

---

## 3. SEO & Metadata Customization (The Reality Check)

Customize the metadata in the Medium settings panel **before** publishing:

- **Display Title:** Keep under 60 characters to prevent truncation on search engine results pages (SERPs).
- **Display Subtitle:** Write a keyword-conscious summary to drive click-through rate (CTR).
- **The Reality Check Rule:** If a user reads *only* the meta-description, they must not feel misled after spending 30 seconds on the page. Conserve the "Trust Multiplier."
- **Custom URL Slug:** Customize the URL slug to contain keyword-rich strings. *Warning: URL slugs cannot be edited after the article goes live.*
- **Canonical URLs:** If cross-posting from a personal blog, set a canonical URL pointing to the original post. This tells search crawlers the primary source is your domain, protecting independent SEO.

---

## 4. Visual Layout Rules

- **Feature Image:** Place a high-resolution feature image directly below the main H1 title.
- **Image Caption:** Attribute all images by linking the caption text to the original source.
- **Image Widths:** Use column-width or mid-width. Avoid "side-straddle" layouts as they wrap text awkwardly and disrupt scrolling on mobile devices.
- **Layout Separation:** Use Kickers for series tags. Use section separators (three dots) to provide eye relief between sections. Avoid drop caps.
